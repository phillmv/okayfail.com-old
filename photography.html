Photography

I am really interested in documentation, and using photography to tell a story and express emotions. At present, most of my work is currently focussed around friends and family.

I keep an instagram feed I update fairly regularly, and a flickr account where I put up photographs not taken on my cellphone.

I have yet to assemble a proper portfolio, but in the meantime please enjoy the following sets:

Portugal series

I spent many years living with my maternal grandparents and in many ways they were my primary role models. Unfortunately, when we moved to Canada I only saw them once a year.

I became acutely aware of how little time we had left together, and eventually I started taking pictures whenever I go back. I have a set for 2008, 2009, 2010, my grandmother's funeral, 2011, summer 2012 and 2012.

Goodbye, seu Vieira

In early 2012, we discovered that my grandfather Diamantino had a terminal form of liver cancer.

At first, we didn't tell him about it. It was hard.

A day in the science

My friend Chris is a linguistics graduate student and otherwise extremely great.

I followed him around for an afternoon as he conducted an experiment in his lab.
